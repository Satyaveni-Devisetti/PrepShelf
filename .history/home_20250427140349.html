About Us

PrepShelf is a dedicated online library designed to support GATE aspirants and engineering students. Our platform offers easy access to a wide range of GATE-oriented and subject-specific books, curated playlists, handwritten notes, and previous year question papers. Students can explore well-organized content tailored to their preparation needs, making learning more efficient and focused. Whether you are just starting or revising, PrepShelf is your trusted companion. Our goal is to simplify your preparation journey by bringing together all essential resources in one place.
