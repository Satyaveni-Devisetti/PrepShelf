What is GATE?

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national-level entrance exam conducted jointly by the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, and seven Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) across the country. It tests the knowledge of candidates in various engineering, technology, and science subjects.

A high GATE score allows candidates to pursue postgraduate programs (M.E./M.Tech./PhD) in prestigious institutes like IITs, NITs, and IISc, and also opens opportunities for recruitment in Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) like ONGC, NTPC, and IOCL.
